About Twendee Majuu

Twendee Majuu is based in Nairobi, Kenya and exists to help Kenyans travel abroad safely, honestly, and with the right guidance.

Who We Are

A trusted travel guidance platform

Twendee Majuu helps Kenyans who are seeking jobs abroad, visa application support, consultation, and honest travel preparation.

We believe that travelling abroad should not be based on confusion, pressure, or false promises. It should be based on proper information, correct documents, and a clear step-by-step process.
