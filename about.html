About Twendee Majuu

Safe travel guidance for Kenyans willing to go abroad.

Twendee Majuu is based in Nairobi, Kenya and exists to help Kenyans travel abroad safely, avoid scams, and move with trusted support and clean digital workflows.

Our mission

To create a reliable digital platform where Kenyans can apply for consultation, upload documents, track progress, and access genuine travel guidance in one place.

Our promise

No shortcuts. No fake promises. Just clear guidance, professional follow-up, and systems that help applicants prepare the right way.

Why this portal matters

  • Applicants keep their details, files, and appointments in one account.
  • Status updates reduce confusion and repeated follow-ups.
  • Jobs and consultation workflows are easier to manage from one portal.
  • GitHub Pages + Firebase keeps the setup simple enough to launch fast.
